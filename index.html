PetJoy can be given at any time of day, with or without food. They can even be given as a treat, once daily. PetJoy products contain all-natural, human-grade (non-synthetic) ingredients that will not have any negative reactions with any medications your pet may be taking.

Our products come with a 2-year shelf life from the expiration date on the bottles (which is the ship by date).
